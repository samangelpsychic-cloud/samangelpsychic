samangelpsychic Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how samangelpsychic handles data for our Pinterest application. samangelpsychic is a personal content-publishing tool used solely to publish original content to the owner's own social media and Pinterest accounts.

What it accesses: Only the account owner's own Pinterest account, with the owner's permission, to create and schedule pins on the owner's own boards.

Data: samangelpsychic does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data from Pinterest users or visitors, and does not access other users' data. Tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer.

Third parties: No data is sold or transferred to third parties.
